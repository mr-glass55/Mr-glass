About Mr. Glass

Designing Interiors with Precision and Passion

Welcome to Mr. Glass, founded by Mr. Shaan, your premier partner for innovative and elegant interior design solutions. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for transforming spaces, we specialize in creating bespoke interiors for mirror houses, companies, and retail shops.

Our philosophy centers on understanding our clients' unique visions and translating them into functional, aesthetic, and inspiring environments. From conceptualization to execution, we blend creativity with practicality, ensuring every project reflects our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction.

Based in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, we are proud to serve clients across the region, bringing dream interiors to life one space at a time.

Mr. Glass, a 22years old business has achieved tremendous growth and is supplying the materials all over India. Every day we are adding new clients and we are providing the best services in the industry.
